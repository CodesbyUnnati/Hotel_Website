About Hotel LD

This is a four star hotel named "Ambience"; now for a period of time it is converted to "LockDown Hotel" to help Indian Government in order to handle the situation rose due to global pandemic Corona Virus.

We provide free services for people struck in states between Delhi. We also provide free food services for beggars once a day. We provide free food and shelter for people on the basis of their booking as per our first come first serve service.
